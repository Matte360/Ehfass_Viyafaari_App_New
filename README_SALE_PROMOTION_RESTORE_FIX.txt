Viyafaari Town - Sale Promotion Restore Fix

This update restores and improves sale promotion display after the cart/tracking UI update.

Fixed/added:
- Today Sale Offers section on client home page.
- Sale item cards show old/before price with red crossed line.
- Sale item cards show current/new price clearly.
- Sale badge shows discount percentage.
- Bulk discount text still appears under sale price.
- Seller dashboard preview cards now also show old price + current price.
- Add/Edit Item page now labels the price as Current / new price when sale promotion is enabled.
- Old price field now says Before / old price and explains it will be crossed out.

Seller reminder:
To show a sale promotion, open Business Portal > Catalog > 3-dot menu > Add sale / discount.
Enable Sale promotion, enter Before/old price higher than Current/new price, then save.
