Open:
android/app/src/main/AndroidManifest.xml

Add these lines below the opening <manifest ...> line and above <application ...>:

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION" />
<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION" />

No additional Android permission is normally required for selecting an image
with the current image_picker package.
